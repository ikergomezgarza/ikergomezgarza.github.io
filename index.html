Relative Valuation / Comps
Automated comps table screening peers by sector, industry, and index via Finviz, filtered by market cap and growth profile. Pulls live EV/EBITDA and EV/Revenue multiples from SEC EDGAR and Alpaca to derive implied valuations across peer groups.

Model 4 · DCFInvestment Banking
Discounted Cash Flow Model
Intrinsic valuation model projecting free cash flows from live SEC EDGAR financials. Computes WACC using a calculated beta against SPY via Alpaca and the 10-year Treasury rate from FRED. Outputs intrinsic price vs market price with a full sensitivity grid across growth and discount rate assumptions.
